Chicken Salad

An image of a chicken salad prepared in a bowl. Credit: https://natashaskitchen.com/chicken-salad-recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chopped Chicken
  • Celery
  • Seedless Grapes
  • Dried Cranberries
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Roasted Pecans
  • Seasonings
  • Celery Leaves

Instructions:

  1. Put your chopped chicken into a large bowl
  2. Then add the celery, grapes, dried cranberries, greek yogurtt, pecans, celery leaves into the bowl
  3. Season lightly with salt and pepper
  4. Stir thoroughly
  5. Your chicken salad is complete! Remember to keep it refridgerated and enjoy your new meal!
